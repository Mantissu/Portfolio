About me

In 2016 my main focus was on becoming a graphic designer. Alongside the main module I stumbled upon IT classes. Learning basics of C++ was what captured my attention but HTML, CSS and JavaScript ended up keeping me there for next 4 years. During my internships in M16 and Presta-Mod the knowledge proved itself to be really helpful. I was trusted not only with design of multiple websites but also creating them, ready to deliver to clients.
